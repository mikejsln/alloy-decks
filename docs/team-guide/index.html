What it is

An agentic venture studio and innovation engine.

It rapidly evaluates every idea, determines the right path, internal or external, and builds the move that creates the most value for Grupo AG: a new venture, an internal build, or a buy, partner, or invest play. 

The idea
Cheap, plentiful ideas in, value for Grupo AG out
Continuous Grupo AG context becomes a steady flow of ideas; each is scored and routed to the path that creates the most value. The funnel stays large, so no single bet is precious: killing is cheap and unemotional.
The substrate
A repo per venture
Each company is its own self-contained, queryable record of every assumption tested and every dollar spent.
Who runs it
A Board, Venture Builders, and an AI Studio Director.
Board The stage-gate decider. Reviews output at Board meetings and chooses what advances at each gate. No day-to-day involvement.
Venture Builders The humans in the loop. Work with the AI daily, steer the work, run the real-world tests it can't (interviews, pilots, LOIs), and feed learnings back. Venture Builders are plug and play.
Studio Director The AI CEO. Runs the fleet of agents, decomposes each request, drafts every artifact, and delivers a daily briefing to the Venture Builders.

The five-lever decision

Any idea can be pursued five ways. A NewCo is for genuine white space.

These five levers are the corporate innovation toolkit. The studio researches, generates, and evaluates every idea, then recommends the lever that wins, reserving a NewCo for genuine white space, when the other four aren't as attractive.

01 Build-in-core Develop it inside a Grupo AG BU that already owns the customer. fit + ownership
BUY / PARTNER / INVEST ("BPI" PATH)
02 Buy Acquire a company that already has it; speed over build. speed
03 Partner A commercial relationship, no equity: Grupo AG as customer or vendor. customer / vendor
04 Invest A minority stake for optionality on an adjacent bet. optionality
05 NewCo Stand up a brand-new external company, for true white space when no other lever is as attractive. white space

The two-pass evidence loop

At each stage he AI drafts, the human proves and refines.

The studio does everything AI can do alone, then hands the Venture Builders the half only a human can do, the real-world tests, and learns from what comes back.

Pass 1 · the AI
Does everything it can alone.
·A soft read, a provisional Continue / Pivot / Kill leaning, never a verdict.
·A Test Plan, the most dangerous assumptions first, each with a pass/fail threshold set in advance.
·An Evidence Kit, everything to run the tests the same day: target lists, screeners, interview guides, pricing and LOI templates.
Pass 2 · the Venture Builder
Proves it in the real world.
·Runs the tests, books and runs the interviews, runs the pricing and pre-sale tests, works the pilot and LOI conversations.
·Captures raw evidence, transcripts, notes, survey results, signed LOIs, internal docs. Raw, not summaries.
·Hands it back through the Project Manager agent, who files the source and structures findings.
Then the studio regenerates a firm decision
The agentic studio AI team re-reads the evidence, update the Investment Memo's Assumptions to Test, and issue one disposition. The AI never fabricates evidence or fakes a verdict; untested assumptions stay untested.

How it gets better over time

The studio runs a kaizen loop every morning.

Before its daily briefing to the Venture Builder, the studio learns from how the Venture Builder edited its outputs and how the work was discussed, then upgrades its own agents, skills, and methodology. Small fixes are applied and logged; bigger changes are proposed for the Venture Builder to approve. It compounds: the studio you use next month is sharper than today's.

Learns from your edits
Every time the Venture Builder rewrites a memo or corrects a call, /studio-improve infers the rule and bakes it in.
Source Steward keeps it current
/source-review finds newer, better data, re-verifies every citation, and cascades the update across all docs.
The roster grows with the work
When a capability gap recurs, the CEO proposes a new AI employee to approve. The swarm has grown to 36 this way.
Always-on outputs
A live portfolio registry, the 9am daily briefing, documented gate verdicts, and the kaizen log + source ledger.
